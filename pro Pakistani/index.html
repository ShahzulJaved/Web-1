Education Department Introduces
New Ex-Pakistan Leave Policy for Teachers

The Higher Education Department has introduced new policy for ex-Pakistan leave for teachers.

Under the policy,teachers seeking study leave must submit a surety bond of Rs.2.5 million.

The Department of Higher Education has issued a notification outlining the new policy for both teachers and employees. Furthermore, the department has requested details of all current cases of ex-Pakistan leave.

Moreover, if a teacher or employee fails to complete their degree after taking study leave, they will be required to return the salary received during the period.
